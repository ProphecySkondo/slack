About OpenScript

OpenScript is a focused community for Roblox developers who want to share and discover client-side scripts. We keep things intentionally simple — only LocalScripts and ModuleScripts are supported, because we believe in clean, transparent, client-run code.

What we support

LocalScript
LocalScript
Runs on the client. Perfect for UI, input handling, and local effects.
ModuleScript
ModuleScript
Reusable modules required by LocalScripts. Shared libraries, utilities, components.
Custom
Custom types
JSON configs, data files, or anything that pairs with your script. You define the extension.

Regular scripts (serverside) are not supported — this keeps the platform safe and focused on what clients can actually run.
